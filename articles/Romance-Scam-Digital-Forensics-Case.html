The New Face of Catfishing

Traditional background checks rarely catch these operators. They use burner phones, VPNs, stolen photos, and carefully curated social media profiles that look completely legitimate. By the time a victim realises something is wrong, thousands — sometimes millions — may already be lost, and legal marriage or asset transfers may be imminent.

How Aspen Meridian Exposes the Truth

Even when chat logs are deleted and profiles are wiped, residual data remains. We recover it, authenticate it, and build an ironclad picture of the real person behind the mask.
