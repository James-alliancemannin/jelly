Immediate protection steps (non-destructive)
  • Do not factory reset devices if you may need evidence preservation (litigation, stalking, fraud).
  • Enable multi-factor authentication (prefer hardware key or authenticator app over SMS where possible).
  • Rotate passwords using a password manager; avoid re-use across services.

What this scan is (and isn’t)
